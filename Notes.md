# ICs

- ITE IT6603E
  - HDMI receiver
- ITE IT6505E
  - DP transmitter
- Megawin MPC89L515AF
  - 8051 MCU
  - http://www.megawin.com.tw/en-global/support/downloadDocument/136/292
- Atmel I2C EEPROM
  - HDMI EDID

# Serial port

- 3V3 logic levels
- ~1 Mbaud, 8N1
- Header JP3
  - TX (DP-out connector side)
  - GND (middle)
  - RX (board-edge side)

# Power

- 5V, 2A power supply
- DC jack
  - Center positive
